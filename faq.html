Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to your questions about Cardni Business solutions

What is Cardni Business? +
Cardni Business is a comprehensive digital voucher distribution platform offering EVD systems, B2B API integrations, and a consumer marketplace.
Where is Cardni based? +
Cardni is headquartered in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, serving businesses across the MENA region.
How do I get started? +
Contact our team through the website form, WhatsApp, or email. We will assess your needs and recommend the best solution.
What is the EVD platform? +
Our Electronic Voucher Distribution platform enables offline voucher sales through POS terminals and retail networks.
Does EVD work offline? +
Yes, our EVD system is designed to work in offline environments with automatic sync when connectivity is restored.
What products can I sell with EVD? +
Mobile recharge, gaming cards, streaming subscriptions, gift cards, and more.
How do I integrate the API? +
Register for an API key, test in our sandbox environment, then go live. Full documentation is provided.
Do you have a Salla plugin? +
Yes, we offer a one-click Salla plugin for instant integration with your Salla store.
What are the API rate limits? +
Rate limits depend on your plan. Contact us for specific details about your integration needs.
How does Cardni Store work? +
Visit cardni.com, browse products, pay securely, and receive your digital code instantly via email.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, STC Pay, and bank transfers.
How does the commission structure work? +
Commissions vary by product category and volume. Higher volumes unlock better rates.
When do I get paid? +
Settlements are processed weekly for EVD partners and monthly for B2B integrators.
