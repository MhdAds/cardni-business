1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account or use our services, we collect personal information including:

  • Full name and contact details
  • Email address and phone number
  • Business information and industry details
  • Payment and billing information
  • Identification documents for verification purposes

Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you interact with our platform:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information and operating system
  • Pages visited and features accessed
  • Timestamps and session duration
  • Click patterns and navigation behavior

Transaction Data

To process payments and transactions, we collect and process:

  • Payment method and transaction history
  • Billing addresses and invoice records
  • Refund and chargeback information

We only collect information necessary to provide and improve our services. We do not collect sensitive data such as racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, or religious beliefs unless explicitly required by law.

2. How We Use Your Information

Service Delivery

We use your information to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Process payments and transactions
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • Provide customer support and troubleshooting
  • Deliver requested features and functionality

Communication

We may contact you for:

  • Service announcements and important updates
  • Password recovery and security alerts
  • Promotional messages and special offers (with your consent)
  • Responding to your inquiries and requests
  • Sending surveys and feedback requests

Service Improvement

We use your information to:

  • Analyze trends and user behavior patterns
  • Develop new features and enhance existing ones
  • Conduct research and analytics
  • Personalize user experience
  • Debug and troubleshoot technical issues

Legal and Compliance

We may use your information to:

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Enforce our terms of service
  • Protect against legal liability and fraud
  • Respond to government requests

3. Data Storage and Security

Encryption and Protection

Your data is protected through multiple security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data transmission
  • SSL/TLS certificates for website security
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Secure authentication mechanisms and password hashing
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

Data Storage Location

Cardni Business stores user data on secure servers located in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA). Our infrastructure complies with international security standards and local regulatory requirements.

Access Controls

We implement strict access controls:

  • Role-based access control (RBAC)
  • Multi-factor authentication for employee accounts
  • Limited employee access to personal data
  • Audit logs for all data access

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. When you request account deletion, we securely erase your data within 30 days, except where legal requirements mandate longer retention.

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. Please use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Service Providers

We share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform and providing services to you. These providers include:

  • Payment processors and financial institutions
  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers
  • Customer support and communication tools
  • Analytics and monitoring services
  • Email delivery and notification services

Data Sharing Restrictions

Cardni Business DOES NOT:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties for marketing or profit
  • Share sensitive financial information without encryption
  • Disclose your data to unverified or unsecured partners
  • Share information without appropriate legal agreements

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to protect our rights:

  • Compliance with court orders or legal subpoenas
  • Responses to government investigations
  • Prevention of fraud or criminal activity
  • Protection of user safety and security

5. Cookies and Tracking

Types of Cookies

Our platform uses different types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies for authentication and session management
  • Performance cookies to measure site usage and performance
  • Preference cookies to remember your settings
  • Marketing cookies for targeted advertising (with consent)

Analytics Tools

We use analytics services to understand how users interact with our platform:

  • Google Analytics for traffic analysis and user behavior
  • Heatmaps and session recordings (anonymized)
  • Conversion tracking for marketing optimization

Opting Out

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser cookie settings and privacy controls
  • Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals
  • Opt-out links provided by third-party services
  • Cookie consent preferences in your account settings

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our platform and prevent you from accessing certain features.

6. Your Rights

Right to Access

You have the right to request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. We will provide this information in a clear, structured, and commonly used format within 30 days of your request.

Right to Correction

You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data. We will update your information promptly and inform relevant service providers of the changes.

Right to Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain exceptions such as legal retention requirements or ongoing disputes. We will complete deletion requests within 30 days where possible.

Right to Data Portability

You can request your personal data in a machine-readable format to transfer to another service provider. We will provide your data in a structured, commonly used, and portable format.

Right to Withdraw Consent

If you have provided consent for specific uses of your data (such as marketing communications), you can withdraw it at any time. This will not affect the legality of processing before withdrawal.

Right to Object

You can object to certain processing activities, including direct marketing, automated decision-making, and profiling. We will honor your objection unless we have a legitimate reason to continue processing.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at:

  • Email: info@cardni.com

  • Through your account settings (where applicable)

We may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request. We will respond to all requests as promptly as possible, and typically within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Cardni Business is not intended for users under the age of 18, or the age of digital consent in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take immediate steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information to us should contact us immediately.

We recommend that parents and guardians take an active role in their children's online activities to protect their privacy and security.

By using Cardni Business, you represent that you are at least 18 years of age or have the legal capacity to enter into a binding agreement.

8. Changes to This Policy

Cardni Business may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the 'Last Updated' date.

For significant changes, we will provide additional notice, such as sending you an email notification or prominently displaying a notice on our website. Your continued use of Cardni Business after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
