Our Story

About Cardni Business

Transforming digital commerce across Saudi Arabia with SAMA-licensed solutions trusted by 500+ merchants

Founded for Growth

In 2021, Cardni Business was born in Riyadh with a clear vision: to simplify how companies distribute digital vouchers and manage customer rewards at scale. What began as an innovative solution has grown into a comprehensive platform serving 500+ merchants across the kingdom and the broader MENA region.

Today, we leverage cutting-edge technology to deliver three core solutions: our Enterprise Voucher Distribution platform for large-scale campaigns, B2B APIs for seamless integrations, and our consumer marketplace connecting millions to exclusive rewards. SAMA licensed and CITC registered, we're committed to driving digital transformation aligned with Saudi Vision 2030.
