About Cardni Business

Empowering businesses with cutting-edge digital voucher solutions across the MENA region

Our Story

Founded in Saudi Arabia, Cardni Business started with a simple vision: to simplify how companies distribute digital vouchers and manage customer rewards at scale. What began as an innovative idea has grown into a comprehensive platform serving hundreds of businesses across the MENA region.

Today, we leverage cutting-edge technology to deliver three core solutions: our Enterprise Voucher Distribution platform for large-scale campaigns, B2B APIs for seamless integrations, and our consumer marketplace connecting millions of customers to exclusive digital rewards. We are committed to driving digital transformation across the region.
